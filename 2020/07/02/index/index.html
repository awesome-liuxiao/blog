About Me

2020-07-02

My name is Liu, Xiao (柳瀟).
I was born in ZhangYe which is a small and beautiful city in Gansu Province, China.
I spent 19 years at ZhangYe with my family untill I graduated from high school.
Then I came to Pusan National University (PNU) at Busan in South Korea for collage in 2009.
During 2009 to 2010, I spent one year on studying Korean and applied Computer Science as my major for undergraduate at PNU.
In August 2014, I recieved my Bachelor of Engineering degree and then started studying at the graduate school of CS department in PNU for getting the Ph.D degree until nowadays.

  • My works
    Nowadays, I’m a Ph.D candidate at Programming Languages Lab. in the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering of PNU. My advisor is Professor Gyun Woo who is an outstanding expert at PLT and Compilers.
    My research interests are Web development, Data & Program analysis, and cloud computing. My Github repositories cotain my on going works and projects. The papers I’ve published are collected on My ResearchGate, my DOI is 0000-0003-3480-3250.
